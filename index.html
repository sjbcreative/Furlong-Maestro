Maestro is widely acknowledged as being ‘best in class’ when it comes to school music administration.

Specifically designed to manage all the challenges of a busy music department, Maestro can not only significantly improve communication with pupils, parents and teaching staff, but as the system is supremely simple to use and hosted in the cloud, you should see financial benefits too.

And all this means you will spend less time on administration and have more time to spend for those extra rehearsals or time with your exam pupils!
